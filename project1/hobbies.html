Lucien Vanias - Hobbies

VIDEO GAMES

Playing video games is easily my favorite thing to do. The video game medium is so unique and interesting; being able to freely explore a fictional world yourself is something that just can't be done in any other kinds of media. Things like movies are limited by time or the plot and often don't get to show you the full picture, while games don't suffer from these restraints at all. The gameplay itself is fun, but many games now tell great stories that rival, or can even surpass the quality of stories seen in traditional mediums.

I've been playing video games since I was 3 years old, and they impacted my life a ton, and for the better. I don't think I would be the same person I am now if I wasn't into video games. Video games have even influenced my career path, and I want to create my own video games someday; I even have some concepts and stories ready and being worked on.

  • Metal Gear Solid Î”: Snake Eater (2025)
